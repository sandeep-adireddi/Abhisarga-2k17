  • Ice Cream Stick Modelling

    You will be given only ice cream sticks (you can use fevikwik and paints also) as material. An elaborate task will be given on the spot. The participants are supposed to use the sticks to build a structure that should achieve the goal. For instance: Build a bridge that holds a standard size mobile phone for at least one whole minute, or build a boat out of the ice cream sticks that will take a mobile phone from one edge of a water body to another. Best two structures will be awarded prizes worth 1000 and 500 rupees.
    Note: Team size of 5 to 7 is allowed.

  • Beg, Borrow, Steal

    You think you’ve got everything you need? Think Again. Prepare to use all your resources and brainpower to fetch the most ridiculous, unfathomable and extraordinary things possible from a list of items given to you. Each object on this list earns you specified points and the team with the highest points wins. Best two teams will win prizes of rupees 1000 and 500. Beware. This event is a race against time and the other teams. Come prepared.
    Note: The registration will be on-spot and on first come basis.    
    Rules: Find as many items on the list as possible within the given time period.
          Team size: 5 people

  • Canvas Painting

    Colours, paints and your artistic hands is all you need for the artsy celebration we have here. A blank canvas – all yours to design and take goodies back for the masterpiece that you create.
    Remember, when we say hands – we genuinely mean HANDS (or legs – if you’re up for it), no brushes or palettes!

  • Eat All You Can

    To satisfy your ravishing hunger, IIITS brings to you this food festival - EAT ALL YOU CAN. We invite all bhukkads, and food is on the house. Before registering though, just keep in mind that the ‘food’ served will be of our choice, and there’s no backing out later.
    Note: Teams are in pairs of 2 – a boy and a girl
    The Jodi that showcases maximum talent and finishes all rounds with clean plates and empty glasses will win 750 bucks each.

  • De'Maske Arte (Face Painting)

    Using your imagination to the fullest, show your creativity while using faces as your canvas sheets and paint your heart out. Unleash the power of your right brain and give us your best strokes. The top two face painting teams will win 1000 and 500 bucks respectively.
    Note: Team size: 4 people

  • Paper Dressing

    This is an event for all the designers and models. Using one of the most used mediums of spreading news, we challenge you to create a front page story yourself by the dress you prepare, tearing and wearing the newspaper to design a dress. Using ribbons, coloured paper, plaster and staplers, create the best dress you can and the top two dresses will win 1000 and 500 bucks respectively.
    Note: Team size: 4 to 5 people per team
